Killing the Duckies

We are here under one united purpose to kill all of the Duckies. These Duckies have had it too good for too long and now we will take them down. The tyranny of them to be at the forefront of all our projects and was even one of the most important questions on our final response.

Eat a carrot

Carrots are good for your eyes. On the other hand, we denounce with righteous indignation and dislike men who are so beguiled and demoralized by the charms of pleasure of the moment, so blinded by desire, that they cannot foresee the pain and trouble that are bound to ensue; and equal blame belongs to those who fail in their duty through weakness of will, which is the same as saying through shrinking from toil and pain. These cases are perfectly simple and easy to distinguish.
